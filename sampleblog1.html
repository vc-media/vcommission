Monetizing High-Intent COD Traffic in 2026

Published on February 5, 2026 | By vCommission Editorial

Cash on Delivery (COD) remains a powerhouse in the Indian e-commerce landscape. For affiliate marketers, understanding how to tap into high-intent traffic for COD offers is the key to massive scaling.

Why COD Conversions are Rising

In 2026, even with the rise of digital payments, a significant portion of the Indian consumer base prefers the security of paying only when the product reaches their doorstep. This reduces the "friction" of a sale, leading to higher conversion rates for affiliates.

Pro Tip: Focus on Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities where COD trust is highest. Use localized ad copy to increase your Click-Through Rate (CTR).

Best Products for COD Marketing

E-commerce categories like Kitchen Accessories and Beauty Wellness perform exceptionally well. Brands like Wonderchef have seen a 40% increase in affiliate-driven COD orders this year alone.
